JavaZone will be held in Oslo Spektrum September 12th-13th 2012. The sale of Early Bird tickets will end on April the 15. and Late Bird tickets will be available untill we are sold out.

You can buy any number of tickets and assign them to co-workers and friends who shall attend the conference. Each individual attendee is responsible for updating his or her contact information. When required information has been registered, the ticket will be sent directly to the attendee.

Tickets for JavaZone 2012 are administrated by Event Systems AS, and can be bought in our webshop
